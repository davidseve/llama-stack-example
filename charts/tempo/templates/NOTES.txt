=== Configure LlamaStack ===

To send traces from LlamaStack to this Tempo instance:

  helm upgrade llama-stack charts/llama-stack \
    --set providers.telemetry.enabled=true \
    --set providers.telemetry.sinks=otel \
    --set providers.telemetry.otlpEndpoint="http://{{ include "tempo.serviceName" . }}:4318"

Or if metrics go to a different collector (e.g., RHOAI):

  --set providers.telemetry.otlpEndpoint="http://rhoai-collector:4318" \
  --set providers.telemetry.otlpTracesEndpoint="http://{{ include "tempo.serviceName" . }}:4318"

=== Configure Grafana ===

  helm upgrade grafana charts/grafana \
    --set tempo.enabled=true \
    --set tempo.url="http://{{ include "tempo.serviceName" . }}:3200"
